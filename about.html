Our Story

What is Wheels of Love?

Wheels of Love is an official program of the Local Government Unit of Bago City, Negros Occidental. Established in 2021, the program was created in response to the growing need for mobility assistance among persons with disabilities (PWDs) in Bago City, particularly among the elderly and low-income communities.

The program provides free wheelchairs and mobility assistance to qualified PWDs, including wheelchair distribution, beneficiary assessment, community outreach, and support services — all at no cost to qualified beneficiaries.

Through partnerships with local health centers, barangay officials, and volunteer medical professionals, Wheels of Love has reached over 1,200 beneficiaries across all 48 barangays of Bago City.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

🎯

Mission

To provide accessible, compassionate, and inclusive mobility assistance to all residents of Bago City — especially persons with disabilities (PWDs) and the underprivileged — through free wheelchairs, community outreach, and support programs that promote independence and dignity.

🌟

Vision

A Bago City where every person with disability has access to mobility support, where no PWD is left behind, and where every resident in need receives a wheelchair and the care they deserve from their community and local government.

💎

Core Values

Compassion — We serve with genuine care and empathy.

Integrity — We act with honesty, transparency, and accountability.

Excellence — We strive for the best health outcomes.

Community — We believe in the power of collective action.

🏛️

LGU Commitment

The Local Government Unit of Bago City fully supports Wheels of Love as a flagship health program, allocating dedicated budget, personnel, and infrastructure. The program operates under the City Health Office with direct oversight from the Office of the Mayor.

What We Offer

Our Services

🫀

ECG Screening

Free electrocardiogram screenings to detect heart irregularities early, available at all partner health centers.

💊

Medicine Assistance

Monthly medicine packs for qualified hypertension and heart disease patients who cannot afford maintenance medications.

🩺

Health Consultations

Free consultations with licensed physicians and cardiologists during scheduled community outreach activities.

📚

Health Education

Regular seminars and workshops on heart-healthy lifestyles, diet, exercise, and stress management for all ages.

🏘️

Community Outreach

Mobile health teams visiting all 48 barangays to bring services directly to residents who cannot travel to health centers.

👴

Senior Care

Specialized care and priority services for senior citizens, including home visits for bedridden elderly patients.

Government Support

LGU Involvement

Wheels of Love operates under the direct supervision of the Bago City Local Government Unit, with full institutional support from the City Mayor's Office and the City Health Office.

The program is funded through the city's annual health budget, supplemented by grants from the Department of Health (DOH) and partnerships with private sector organizations committed to community health.

Barangay Health Workers (BHWs) and Barangay Health Emergency Response Teams (BHERTs) serve as the program's frontline implementers, ensuring services reach every corner of the city.
