Our Story

What is Wheels of Love?

Wheels of Love is an official program of the Local Government Unit of Bago City, Negros Occidental. Established in 2021, the program was created in response to the growing need for mobility assistance among persons with disabilities (PWDs) in Bago City, particularly among the elderly and low-income communities.

The program provides free wheelchairs and mobility assistance to qualified PWDs, including wheelchair distribution, beneficiary assessment, community outreach, and support services — all at no cost to qualified beneficiaries.

Through partnerships with local health centers, barangay officials, and volunteer medical professionals, Wheels of Love has reached over 1,200 beneficiaries across all 48 barangays of Bago City.

✅

✅

Regular community outreach in all 48 barangays of Bago City

✅

Coordination with barangay officials to identify and assist PWDs in all communities

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

🎯

Mission

To provide accessible, compassionate, and inclusive mobility assistance to all residents of Bago City — especially persons with disabilities (PWDs) and the underprivileged — through free wheelchairs, community outreach, and support programs that promote independence and dignity.

🌟

Vision

A Bago City where every person with disability has access to mobility support, where no PWD is left behind, and where every resident in need receives a wheelchair and the care they deserve from their community and local government.

💎

Core Values

Compassion — We serve with genuine care and empathy.

Integrity — We act with honesty, transparency, and accountability.

Excellence — We strive for the best health outcomes.

Community — We believe in the power of collective action.

🏛️

LGU Commitment

The Local Government Unit of Bago City fully supports Wheels of Love as a flagship PWD mobility program, allocating dedicated budget, personnel, and infrastructure. The program operates under the City Social Welfare and Development Office with direct oversight from the Office of the Mayor.

What We Offer

Our Services

♿

Wheelchair Distribution

Free wheelchairs provided to qualified PWDs across all 48 barangays of Bago City.

🔍

Beneficiary Assessment

Thorough assessment of PWD needs to ensure the right mobility aid is provided to each beneficiary.

🤝

Community Outreach

Mobile teams visiting all barangays to identify and assist PWDs who cannot travel to the program office.

📋

PWD Registration

Assistance with PWD ID registration and documentation to help beneficiaries access government services.

🏘️

Barangay Coordination

Working with barangay officials to identify and prioritize the most vulnerable PWDs in each community.

👴

Senior PWD Care

Specialized assistance for elderly persons with disabilities, including home visits for bedridden seniors.

Government Support

LGU Involvement

Wheels of Love operates under the direct supervision of the Bago City Local Government Unit, with full institutional support from the City Mayor's Office and the City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO).

The program is funded through the city's annual social welfare budget, supplemented by grants from the Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD) and partnerships with private sector organizations committed to PWD inclusion.

Barangay officials and Social Welfare Officers serve as the program's frontline implementers, ensuring wheelchairs and mobility assistance reach every PWD in need across all 48 barangays.
