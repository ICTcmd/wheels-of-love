## ✨ **Key Improvements**

### **1. 📱 Enhanced Mobile Navigation**
- ✅ **Slide-in menu** from the right side (280px width)
- ✅ **Smooth hamburger animation** (transforms to X when open)
- ✅ **Dark overlay** behind menu for better focus
- ✅ **Body scroll lock** when menu is open
- ✅ **Close on overlay click** or ESC key
- ✅ **Auto-close** on link click or orientation change

### **2. 👆 Better Touch Interactions**
- ✅ **44x44px minimum touch targets** (accessibility standard)
- ✅ **Touch feedback** on buttons and cards
- ✅ **Swipe gestures** for gallery/lightbox navigation
- ✅ **Prevent double-tap zoom** on buttons
- ✅ **Smooth scroll** for anchor links
- ✅ **Pull-to-refresh** visual feedback

### **3. 🎨 Improved Design**
- ✅ **Larger, readable text** (16px minimum to prevent iOS zoom)
- ✅ **Better spacing** and padding throughout
- ✅ **Sticky header** that stays visible while scrolling
- ✅ **Hidden topbar** on mobile for cleaner look
- ✅ **Full-width buttons** for easier tapping
- ✅ **Optimized card layouts** for small screens

### **4. 📝 Enhanced Forms**
- ✅ **Larger input fields** (16px font to prevent zoom)
- ✅ **Better focus states** with colored borders
- ✅ **Proper keyboard types** (email, tel, etc.)
- ✅ **Viewport lock prevention** during input focus
- ✅ **Improved textarea** with better sizing

### **5. 🖼️ Better Media Display**
- ✅ **Responsive images** with proper aspect ratios
- ✅ **Lazy loading** support for better performance
- ✅ **Optimized gallery grid** (2 columns on mobile, 1 on small)
- ✅ **Swipeable lightbox** for easy navigation
- ✅ **Larger touch targets** for lightbox controls

### **6. ⚡ Performance Optimizations**
- ✅ **GPU-accelerated animations** for smooth transitions
- ✅ **Optimized scroll performance** with requestAnimationFrame
- ✅ **Reduced repaints** with CSS containment
- ✅ **Passive event listeners** where possible
- ✅ **Efficient intersection observers** for lazy loading

### **7. ♿ Accessibility Enhancements**
- ✅ **Focus-visible states** for keyboard navigation
- ✅ **Reduced motion support** for users who prefer it
- ✅ **High contrast mode** support
- ✅ **Proper ARIA labels** on interactive elements
- ✅ **Screen reader optimizations**

### **8. 📲 Device-Specific Features**
- ✅ **iOS safe area support** for notched devices (iPhone X+)
- ✅ **Orientation change handling**
- ✅ **Mobile device detection** (adds `.is-mobile` class)
- ✅ **iOS-specific fixes** (adds `.is-ios` class)
- ✅ **Landscape mode optimizations**
