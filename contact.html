Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can avail of Wheels of Love services? +
All residents of Bago City can avail of our services. Priority is given to senior citizens, persons with disabilities, and low-income households. You will need to present a valid Bago City residency document or barangay certificate.
How do I register as a beneficiary? +
You can register at your nearest Barangay Health Center or visit the Wheels of Love Program Office at Bago City Hall. Bring a valid ID and proof of residency. Online registration will be available soon through this website.
Are all services really free? +
Yes. All Wheels of Love services — including ECG screenings, consultations, and medicine assistance — are completely free for qualified beneficiaries. This is funded by the Bago City LGU and the Department of Health.
How can I volunteer or partner with Wheels of Love? +
We welcome volunteers, medical professionals, and organizations who want to support our mission. Please send us a message through the contact form above or email us directly at heartwarriors@bago.gov.ph.
