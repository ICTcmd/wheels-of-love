Our Foundation

Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To provide accessible, compassionate, and inclusive mobility assistance to all residents of Bago City — especially persons with disabilities (PWDs) and the underprivileged — through free wheelchairs, community outreach, and support programs that promote independence and dignity.

🌟

Our Vision

A Bago City where every person with disability has access to mobility support, where no PWD is left behind, and where every resident in need receives a wheelchair and the care they deserve from their community and local government.

💪

Core Values

Compassion — We serve with genuine care.
Integrity — We act with honesty and transparency.
Excellence — We strive for the best outcomes.
Community — We believe in the power of togetherness.

🏛️

LGU Commitment

The Local Government Unit of Bago City fully supports Wheels of Love as a flagship health program, allocating resources, personnel, and infrastructure to ensure sustainable and impactful service delivery to all barangays.
